About Us

Bookhaven Book Store is a locally-owned spot that carries a wide variety of literature, from ancient text to classic tales to futuristic sci-fi. Our goal is to provide a space where readers of all ages can come together in a tight-knit community and discover their next favorite book
Bookhaven Book Store has been serving readers for over 20 years. Our oldest and primary location, in Charlotte NC, has been the heart of the local reading community. We are passionate in supporting local authors, and host reading clubs to ensure everybody has the best experience
