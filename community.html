Clubs and Activities

At Bookhaven, we host many book clubs and activities to bring our community together. If you enjoy mysteries, fantasy, or poetry, then there is a group for you.
Mystery Book Club
We meet every Tuesday at 6pm. Join us as we explore exciting thrillers and classic mysteries.
Fantasy Circle
We meet every Thursday at 7pm. A community for fans of anything fantasy, from high to low to dark to epic.
Poetry Night
We meet every Saturday at 2pm. Bring your own or enjoy the words of others in a welcoming space.
